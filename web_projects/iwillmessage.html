iWillMessage

iWillMessage was born out of a season of despair and fear. The world endured so much loss during the Covid 19 pandemic and it was during that time, many of us realized how valuable our connections to our loved ones is. Too many people witnessed otherwise healthy loved ones slip away.

Loved ones who were unprepared to say goodbye suddenly found themselves alone. Imagine a world where we could do more and leave our loved ones with pieces of us regardless of the circumstances that led to our departure? What if we could continue the work of developing, building, and nurturing meaningful relationships even after our death? iWillMessage believes that we can empower everyone with a profound solution.
